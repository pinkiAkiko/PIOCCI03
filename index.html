PIOCCI is a chamber for the global Indian business community.

PIOCCI is a unique chamber of energetic business leaders fostering collaboration among People of Indian Origin, Non-Resident Indians, Indian and foreign businesses — a registered society of India since 2003 with a Secretariat at the Diplomatic Enclave in New Delhi.

From Pravasi Bharatiya Divas to bilateral trade missions and policy roundtables, PIOCCI works alongside the Ministry of External Affairs, Invest India and global chambers to open doors for Indian-origin enterprise across 50+ countries.
